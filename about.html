About Xplore

Foreign Observer Xplore is a project by the Foreign Observer that aims to explore issues and trends affecting international students in the U.S. through data analysis and visualization. We welcome feedback about your experience with our projects and ideas to make our project better and more useful to you. If you think of something you'd like to see on Xplore, send us a tip to foreignobserver.neu (a) gmail.com.
